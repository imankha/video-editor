**"Save your best moments from the season before you lose the videos"**

1. **Hook** — "Save your best moments from the season before you lose the videos"
2. **Demo clip** — Embedded/linked Reel Ballers clip of Mehdi (from T1970) showing the finished product

4. **CTA** — "Try it free" link to app.reelballers.com
5. **Personal touch** — Coming from Iman, keep it authentic/casual (not corporate)
