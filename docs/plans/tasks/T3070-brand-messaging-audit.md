# T3070: Brand Messaging Audit & Improvement

## Summary

Audit and improve brand communication across all initial user touchpoints. Replace generic/limited messaging with the high concept: **"Learn From, Preserve and Share Your Athlete's Moments."**

## Problem

Current messaging is inconsistent and undersells the product:
- **Emails**: Share emails, invite emails, and other transactional emails don't explain what Reel Ballers is or does. Recipients have no context.
- **Preloading/onboarding**: App doesn't communicate its value proposition while users wait during loading states.
- **Landing page**: Hero copy ("Higher quality highlights in minutes") is too narrow -- focuses on one feature (quality) instead of the full value (learn, preserve, share).

## Scope

Audit and update messaging at every initial touchpoint:

### 1. Transactional Emails (Resend)
- Share notification emails (when someone shares a reel/game with you)
- Invite emails (referral flow)
- Any other outbound emails
- Each email should include a brief explanation of what Reel Ballers does
- Consistent branding, tone, and CTA

### 2. App Preloading / Onboarding
- Loading screens / splash states should communicate value
- First-time user experience messaging
- Any interstitial states where user is waiting

### 3. Landing Page
- Replace "Higher quality highlights in minutes" with new high concept
- Ensure all copy reinforces: Learn From, Preserve, Share
- Review subheadings, feature descriptions, CTA text for consistency

### 4. Shared Video Pages
- Non-user landing experience on /shared/:token
- Signup CTA messaging
- Value proposition for recipients who aren't yet users

## High Concept

**"Learn From, Preserve and Share Your Athlete's Moments"**

Three pillars:
- **Learn From**: Annotations, playback, coaching tools -- review and improve
- **Preserve**: Auto-export, My Reels, storage -- moments don't disappear with the phone video
- **Share**: Team sharing, shared links, invite flow -- celebrate and collaborate

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All transactional emails include Reel Ballers explanation and branding
- [ ] Loading/preloading states communicate app value
- [ ] Landing page hero uses new high concept (not "Higher quality highlights in minutes")
- [ ] All copy across touchpoints is consistent with the three pillars
- [ ] Shared video pages explain Reel Ballers to non-users
- [ ] No touchpoint leaves a new user wondering "what is this?"
