## Problem

Need to reach highly engaged soccer parents who have enough technical ability to adopt a video editing tool. Organic growth is slow; the right influencer endorsement puts us in front of exactly our target audience.

## Solution

1. **Research phase:** Identify top influencers that youth soccer parents watch — coaches, trainers, soccer parent vloggers, youth development commentators. Focus on those who already discuss video technology, game film, or player development tools.
2. **Outreach strategy:** Define partnership tiers (free account, sponsored post, affiliate). Craft pitch that emphasizes how parents can create highlight reels for their kids.
3. **Execution:** Reach out, provide demo accounts, coordinate content.

## Target Audience Profile

- Parents of youth soccer players (ages 8-18)
- Highly engaged in their child's sports development
- Comfortable with technology (smartphone video recording, basic app usage)
- Active on YouTube, Instagram, TikTok (soccer parent communities)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] List of 10+ influencers with audience size, platform, and relevance score
- [ ] Outreach template/pitch crafted
- [ ] Partnership tier defined (what we offer, what we ask)
- [ ] First round of outreach sent
