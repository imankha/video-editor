## Problem

New users don't understand the workflow (upload game, annotate clips, frame, overlay, export). No onboarding content exists. Users who figure it out love the product; the drop-off is in comprehension, not quality.

## Solution

Record a walkthrough video covering the full pipeline: upload a game, annotate clips with tags/stars, frame and crop, add overlays, export and download from gallery. Keep it under 2-3 minutes. Host on YouTube/Vimeo for embedding on landing page and in-app.
